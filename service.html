Booking your Shoot

We are so excited to capture your upcoming trip memories! We are here to help you plan your fabulous shoot and assist you with any questions you may have along the way. Below, find our 7-step guide on how to book your shoot.

Home Services

1. Inquire

Send in an inquiry to check availability for your travel dates.

2. Receive Confirmation

Within 24-48 hours, we will let you know if your preferred photographer is available for your dates, and if not, can help you find another.

3. Book your Shoot

Once a photographer is confirmed as available, you can officially book and reserve your shoot.

4. Photographer Recommendations

Your photographer will respond with their best recommendations for a route and exact start time for best lighting.

5. Finalise Shoot Plan

We will confirm the game plan with you, and once it's finalised, we will send you a summary with all of your session details, including your meeting spot and photographerâ€™s contact number.

6. Photo Shoot Day!

Meet up with your photographer ten minutes before your shoot to go over your goals, and then it's all about having fun on your session! Within five days after your shoot, you will receive your beautiful gallery in your inbox.
